About Raven Heating & Cooling

Raven Heating & Cooling is a family-owned and operated business located in Tucson, Arizona. Since we are a family-owned business, we believe in treating you like family, too, We have set a standard on professionalism, quality, and customer satisfaction. Here at Raven Heating & Cooling, your satisfaction and comfort are our top priority.

Raven Heating & Cooling has been providing HVAC repair and maintenance solutions right here in Tucson, AZ since 2010. The owner, Matthew Uselton, has been working in the HVAC repair industry for more than 20 years, after learning his trade from his grandfather - who was akso a technician and business owner in Tucson. Matthew's passion for waht he does - both in helping others and in repairing and maintaining HVAC systems - drives every facet of his business. Raven Heating & Cooling puts you - the customer - first.
